Workshop Overview

Motivation, themes, topics of interest, and submission instructions.

Workshop

Our societies face major global challenges to sustain and develop our civilisations. The development of dedicated Intelligent Decision-Support Systems (IDSSs) can help address them. This workshop focuses on how Data Science (DS), Machine Learning (ML), Multi-objective Optimisation (MOO), and Multi-criteria Decision-Analysis/Making (MCDA/MCDM) can support policy and decision-making.

Submission Instructions
  • We invite new ideas, positional statements, reviews/summaries/comments, and techniques on societal challenges.
  • Submissions should be extended abstracts (200–300 words).
  • Please email your submission to: dsmlossf@gmail.com

Important Note: PPSN 2022 is “on-site” only. In case of pandemic-caused restrictions, the event will switch to full online.

Techniques
  • Trade-offs under competing objectives
  • Uncertainty & time-varying policies
  • Visualising trade-offs
  • Surrogate-assisted exploration
  • Interactive decision support
  • Transfer learning & optimization
  • MCDA/MCDM for IDSS
  • Society models: networks/multilayer
Societal Challenges
  • Climate change impacts
  • Public health
  • Urbanization & population growth
  • Poverty & development
  • Infrastructure & waste management
  • Natural & man-made disasters
